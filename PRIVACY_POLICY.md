# Privacy Policy

Universal Plugin Manager With NotePad++ does not collect personal data by default.

## What We Collect
- No telemetry or analytics are collected by the Software.
- Plugins may collect data only with clear disclosure and consent.

## Storage
- Local settings are stored on your device (e.g., `settings.json`, `approved_plugins.json`).

## Third-Party Plugins
- Third-party plugins are subject to their own privacy practices.
- Only approved plugins can be loaded; report concerns via GitHub.

## Changes
- Updates to this policy will be published on the official GitHub repository.
