# Branding Guidelines

## Name and Attribution
- Product name: "Universal Plugin Manager With NotePad++".
- Include attribution: "Universal Plugin Manager With NotePad++".

## Plugin Naming
- Use descriptive names; avoid confusingly similar names to official plugins.
- Do not imply endorsement unless explicitly approved.

## Logos and Marks
- Do not use logos or trademarks without written permission.
- Screenshots of the app are permitted with attribution.

## Descriptions
- Accurately describe plugin capabilities; disclose limitations and requirements.

## Violations
- Misuse may result in removal of approval and other enforcement.
